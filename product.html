Delivery Information

We deliver to all regions in Ghana. Whether you are in Accra, Kumasi, Tamale, Takoradi, or any other city and town, we can get your order to you.

Delivery timeline

Accra & nearby: 1–3 business days via our courier partners.
Other regions: 3–7 business days depending on location and courier service.

Delivery cost

Delivery fees are calculated at checkout based on your location and the item size.

Order tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via WhatsApp. You can use this to monitor your package in real time.

Cash on delivery

We offer cash on delivery in select areas within Accra. A small deposit may be required to confirm the order. Contact us for availability in your area.

Delivery issues

If your package has not arrived within the estimated timeframe, please contact us on WhatsApp with your order reference — we will track it down immediately.

Return Policy

Return window

You have 7 days from the date of delivery to request a return. Requests made after 7 days may not be accepted.

Condition requirements

To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in its original packaging with all accessories and tags intact.

Non-returnable items

The following items cannot be returned: screen protectors (due to hygiene reasons), customised or special-order items, and digital products (e.g. software, gift cards).

How to initiate a return

Contact us on WhatsApp with your order reference number and a brief description of the issue. Our team will guide you through the next steps.

Refund process

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days via your original payment method.

Shipping costs

Return shipping costs are borne by the buyer unless the return is due to an error on our part (wrong item sent, defective product).

Exchange policy

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different item, please initiate a return and place a new order.
